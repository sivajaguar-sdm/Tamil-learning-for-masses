1. Capture one or more label photos

Take close photos of the printed date. You can add up to three photos and combine the OCR results.

Drag across the image to draw a crop box. Drag again to replace it.
3. Enhance and read the cropped label

Smart OCR reads original, enlarged, grayscale and high-contrast versions, then combines the detected expiry patterns.

4. Voice date entry

Voice recognition works best in Chrome. You can also type the phrase manually and tap Use text.
